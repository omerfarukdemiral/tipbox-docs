TIPBOX Chain

The TIPBOX Chain represents the blockchain infrastructure that powers Tipbox's decentralized features and ensures secure, transparent operations.

The TIPBOX Chain is built on:

  • Ethereum compatibility
  • Proof of Stake consensus
  • EVM compatibility
  • Layer 2 scaling solutions
  • Cross-chain interoperability
